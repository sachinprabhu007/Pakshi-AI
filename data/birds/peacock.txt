Name: Peacock

Scientific Name: Pavo cristatus

Habitat:
Forests, grasslands and agricultural land.

Diet:
Seeds, insects and small reptiles.

Conservation Status:
Least Concern

Description:
The Indian Peacock is a large and colorful bird known for its iridescent tail feathers.

Interesting Facts:
- National bird of India.
- Males display their tail feathers during courtship.
